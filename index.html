About Us

PepsiCo is a global leader in the food and beverage industry, offering a wide range of products that include snacks, beverages, and healthier food options. Founded in 1965 through the merger of Pepsi-Cola and Frito-Lay, PepsiCo has grown to become one of the world's most recognizable brands.
Key Facts: Founding Year: 1965 (Merger of Pepsi-Cola and Frito-Lay) Headquarters: Purchase, New York, USA CEO: Ramon Laguarta Employees: Over 260,000 worldwide Global Presence: Operates in more than 200 countries and territories
Products and Brands: PepsiCo's diverse portfolio includes some of the most iconic brands in the world: Beverages: Pepsi, Mountain Dew, Gatorade, Tropicana, Aquafina Snacks: Lay's, Doritos, Cheetos, Ruffles, Quaker Healthier Options: Naked Juice, Sabra, and a growing line of better-for-you snacks and beverages
Commitment to Sustainability: PepsiCo is committed to sustainable growth, aiming to make a positive impact on society and the environment through: Performance with Purpose: Integrating sustainability into business strategy Water Stewardship: Conserving water resources and promoting water security Packaging Innovation: Working towards 100% recyclable, compostable, or biodegradable packaging by 2025
Community and Social Responsibility: PepsiCo actively supports communities worldwide through initiatives that promote education, health, and environmental sustainability. The company also focuses on diversity, equity, and inclusion within its workforce and business operations. Innovation and Future Outlook: Continuously innovating, PepsiCo explores new flavors, packaging, and partnerships to meet changing consumer preferences and environmental challenges. With a commitment to delivering sustainable growth and shareholder value, PepsiCo remains a leader in the global food and beverage industry.

Our Services

Beverage Innovation

At PepsiCo, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional beverage experiences that captivate and refresh consumers worldwide. Our extensive portfolio includes iconic brands such as Pepsi, Mountain Dew, and Gatorade, each crafted to meet diverse tastes and preferences. Whether you crave the classic cola taste of Pepsi or the energizing burst of Mountain Dew, our beverages are designed to inspire moments of joy and refreshment. We continuously innovate, introducing new flavors and formulations that push the boundaries of taste and quality. With a commitment to sustainability and consumer well-being, we strive to deliver products that not only satisfy but also enrich lives.

Snack Excellence

PepsiCo is synonymous with snacks that excite and satisfy consumers globally. From the irresistible crunch of Lay's potato chips to the bold flavors of Doritos and the cheesy goodness of Cheetos, our snack brands are beloved for their quality and taste. We pride ourselves on innovation, constantly introducing new snack experiences that cater to evolving consumer preferences. Beyond flavor, we prioritize nutrition, offering healthier snack options under brands like Quaker and Naked Juice. With a focus on sustainability, we're working towards packaging solutions that minimize environmental impact, ensuring our snacks are enjoyed responsibly. Join us in celebrating the joy of snacking with products that deliver on taste, quality, and sustainability.

Health and Wellness Initiatives

PepsiCo is dedicated to promoting health and wellness through our extensive range of products and initiatives. We understand the importance of offering choices that support balanced lifestyles and nutrition. Our commitment extends beyond traditional snacks and beverages to include a variety of healthier options that cater to diverse consumer needs.
